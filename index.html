In 2022 an idea was revealed to create something fresh, something that people do not think too much about and that is how Deco Inside starts to growing step by step.
Deco Inside will take away the worries of not knowing where to start to decorate your home, thus helping you to solve the problem of not having decoration inspiration, not knowing where to get the different furniture, not knowing the budget that such decoration would cost and also not having knowledge of reliable suppliers to buy such products, because with our web application you do not need to worry about that anymore.

About the project

What is Deco Inside?

Deco Inside is a web application that will help you find your new home renovation design, providing you with the ease of finding inspiration, suppliers, prices, decorations according to your budget, and more, all in one application.

How entrepreneurs are part of Deco Inside?

Because as Deco Inside we also open the doors to medium-sized entrepreneurs by giving them the opportunity to use our platform as a means of advertising to be able to publicize their business along with their decoration products. So, they are available to upload their products and manage their sales through our dashboard.

What are our projections?

Deco Inside's business model is scalable to multiple countries, making global expansion a key future goal. Additionally, we plan to expand our functionalities to offer an even more personalized experience. We will introduce artificial intelligence to create a user community where ideas can be shared. Simultaneously, we also plan to make your dream come true by being able to visualize your home decor not just in the 3D editor but also in a virtual space, like VR or the Metaverse.

Detailed budget

We help you plan your expenses, so you do not have unpleasant surprises and you will have an estimate of what such decoration would cost.

Product list

We offer you a curated selection of products for your decoration.

Favorite list

We give you the opportunity to save those specific products on a favorite list that creates an individual budget for it.

Suppliers

We work with trusted local brands to ensure the quality of each product.

3D editor

We offer you the special possibility of viewing your chosen decoration in a 3D design, so you can change and add the furniture you want.

Decorations request

You will have the opportunity to request 5 personalized designs that you can choose from and get all the information that you need about that decoration.
